Smoky Mountains 2022

Back in the mountains!

We made it back out to the Smokies again for fall break this year (2022). As we have all now been fully vaccinated and (bivalent) boosted, we stayed a little closer to town this time. The trip was short but lots of fun!

Highlights include hiking to the top of Clingman's Dome twice! The first time we hiked straight into cloud! We did another hike around the top of the mountain for a couple of hours until the weather cleared, and then made our way back up to the top via the Appalachian trail. The hike was worth it!

Speaking of "worth it," the next day we did one long hike up to the Alum Cave bluff. This was a new trail for us, and definitely worth it. You started in a southern forest with thick magnolia, and over a few miles and a few thousand feet of elevation traverse through eastern hardwood forests into balsam fir. Awesome!
